HOTELS

Bete Serkie Lalibela

Bete Serkie Lalibela is a hotel located in Lalibela, Ethiopia, near the famous rock-hewn churches. It offers cozy and comfortable accommodation with free breakfast, free WiFi, free parking, and a garden. It is a family-run business that aims to make your experience memorable. The hotel has a restaurant that serves African cuisine, and some rooms have a balcony with a city view.

Services

  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Swimming Pool
  • Spa and Wellness Center
  • Restaurant and Bar
  • 24-hour Room Service
Rating: ★★★★★

Leva resort

LEVA Afar Semera Resort is a 4-star hotel located near the Semera International Airport in Ethiopia. It offers comfortable rooms, free WiFi, a spa, a rooftop pool, and four restaurants. It is the only upscale resort in the area and caters to business travelers, tourists, and locals alike.
